NAME
    play - start a ping pong game session

SYNOPSIS
    play local
    play online

DESCRIPTION
    Launches a ping pong game session.
    Specify 'local' to play against AI or another local player.
    Specify 'online' to connect and play against another online user.

NOTE
    Login is required for online play.
